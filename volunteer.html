In-Home Support Services

Living independently in one's own home is a vital component of the NDIS program, and our In-Home Support Services make this a reality.

Each service is tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals, providing help with tasks that may be challenging due to disabilities.

Passion Plus offers a variety of household tasks on a daily or routine basis, customized to suit your preferences and specific needs. Rest assured, we are always here to assist you. Some of the in-home tasks we can support you with include, but are not limited to:

  • Meal Preparation/Cooking
  • Daily personal care support and dress-up
  • Cleaning and Laundry assistance
  • Shopping Activities
  • Attending Appointment/Errand assistance
  • Welfare/Emotional Checks - Routine or regular welfare check and offering encouragement.

Additionally, we support domestic chores, including Grooming, Dressing, Showering, etc.
