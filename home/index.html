Who We Are

Passion Plus Disability Services Pty Ltd is a compassionate and dedicated provider established with a passion to providing high-quality support and care for individuals with disabilities.

Our team is composed of skilled professionals who are genuinely passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of our clients.

We are driven by a genuine commitment to fostering an inclusive community where everyone is valued and respected.

Whether you are seeking assistance with daily living activities, community participation, or specialized services, we tailor our services to meet the unique needs and goals of each individual.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We empower our client to make informed choices and provide supports they need to achieve their personal goals and independence.
  • Inclusivity: We create a welcoming environment where everyone is valued for their uniqueness and celebrate diversity.
  • Respect: We treat every individual with respect and dignity, recognizing the unique abilities and potential of each individual.
  • Integrity: We maintain the highest standards of integrity and transparency in all our interactions and services.
  • Innovation: We are committed to continuous improvement and innovative approaches to service delivery.
  • Collaboration: We work closely with clients, families, and communities to achieve shared goals.
Care & Support specific to your needs and choices

Why you should consider Passion Plus

We believe everyone deserves to live the life they desire, and we are supporting you in making that a reality. At Passion Plus, it is a service driven by Passion and Commitment to providing high quality care and support that meet the needs of every person living with a disability.

PERSON-CENTERED APPROACH

We are aware that every individual have choices and preferences, we will develop personalized support plans tailored to your specific needs, preferences and goals.

EMPOWERMENT AND INDEPENDENCE

Your choices and preferences are prioritized as we assist you in making informed decisions for your well-being. Our care and support are aligned to your specific needs and choices, focusing on what matters to you and meeting your needs.

24/7 ACCESS TO SUPPORT TEAM

We would support you round the clock and as per your choice. There is always someone you can talk to.

Families and Support Network Involvement

We recognize the importance of families and friends in our life. With your consent, we ensure their involvement in your support decision-making processes and to promote connections with your loved ones.

FLEXIBLE IN OUR SERVICES DELIVERY

We offer flexible options and responsive services enabling you to request adjustments to the supports your receive to meet your changing needs. This may include communication method, changes to services package and times of services delivery etc

FOCUS ON OUTCOMES & QUALITY

It is not just providing supports, but providing services that meet quality standards and this is our target. We prioritize the outcomes every individuals want to achieve and work collaboratively to develop strategies for success.

OUR DRIVING FORCE

A commitment to reach the unreached, providing empathetic and compassionate care and supporting every individual we serve to live their everyday life in the best ways possible.
