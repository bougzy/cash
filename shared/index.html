Shared or Group Living Arrangement

In a Shared or Group Living Arrangement, two or more individuals live together, sharing common living spaces while also having their own private areas.

It provides support for individuals with disabilities to live more independently and socialise while still having access to assistance with daily tasks.

Benefit of shared or group living arrangement may include:
  • Reduced Living Costs
  • Reduced loneliness and isolation by providing opportunities for interaction and friendship.
  • Participant can receive personalised support with daily tasks and personal care, promoting overall healthy living.
  • Community Involvement that promote development of life skills.

Reach out to us to discuss your specific support need, we are more than happy to work with you to make it work
