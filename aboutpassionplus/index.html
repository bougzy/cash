About Passion Plus

Passion Plus Disability Services Pty Ltd is a compassionate provider established with a passion to providing high-quality support and care for individuals with disabilities. Our team is composed of experienced professionals who are passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of our clients. We are driven by a genuine commitment to fostering an inclusive community where everyone is valued and respected.

Whether you are seeking assistance with In-Home daily living supports, Community Participation, Supported Independent Living, Transport Assistance etc, we are here to help you.

Our approach is person-centred, ensuring we meet the needs and goals of each individual while empowering them to live to their fullest potential.

At Passion Plus, we believe in the power of collaboration and work closely with families and caregivers to create a supportive network that truly enhances the quality of life of those we serve. Join us in our mission to build a brighter, more inclusive future for all.

Our Commitment

We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation in service delivery.

Our team regularly undergoes training and development to stay abreast of the latest practices in disability support.

We actively seek feedback from our clients and their families to ensure we are meeting their needs effectively.
