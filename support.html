Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Passion Plus works collaboratively with participants, their families and other support network to provides person-centered care that is specifically tailored to meet the unique needs of each client living with a disability.

Whether you want to live in shared housing with other participants or on your own, we are able to provide your assisted living supports.

This may include help or supervision with daily tasks like personal care, meal preparation, other household tasks, participation in the community, getting ready for the day etc.
